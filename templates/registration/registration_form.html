Player Registration

As a player you need to register with Woodkirk Valley FC which this gives you and your manager the ability to track your progress. You can see your Progress in 'My Badges' and when you completed a badge your manager will apply it to your profile.

As a club 'Child Welfare' is a massive priority so to safeguard your children's data we will only ask for the minimal amount of personal data to keep the website working.

When registering please keep your username a password safe.
